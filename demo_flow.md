# WanderVoice — Demo Storyboard: "The Emerald Whisper"

**Director's Note:** This demo captures the "Wander tempo" in the heart of Bangkok. The transition from the bustling city noise to the sacred, golden atmosphere of Wat Phra Kaew is the emotional anchor.

---

## The Situation
*   **Persona:** Elena, a curious solo traveler.
*   **Location:** Bangkok, Thailand. Standing near the entrance of **Wat Phra Kaew** (Grand Palace).
*   **Time:** Mid-morning, bright tropical sun, the sound of distant bells and many languages.

---

## SCENE 1: The First Breath
**Context:** Elena is standing in the shade of a tree outside the temple. She opens WanderVoice.

| Time | Visual (Screen ID) | Action / Interaction | Audio / Atmosphere |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 0:00 | **Splash** (`splash`) | Fade in: Deep Canopy background. The logo pulses gently. | Traffic hum, distant birds. |
| 0:05 | **Permissions** (`perms`) | Elena taps "Begin your walk". Allows location. | A soft temple bell chime. |
| 0:10 | **Style Selection** (`s5a`) | Elena chooses **"Story"**. Progress bar moves. | "Immersive storytelling mode active." |
| 0:15 | **Setup** (`s7a`) | Enables "Auto-audio" and "Leading cues". | "The city is ready to speak." |
