# WanderVoice — Core Features

WanderVoice is an AI audio guide that tells you stories about where you are. It turns every walk into an interactive journey.

## 1. Smart Audio Guide
*   **Location-Aware**: Automatically tells stories based on exactly where you are standing.
*   **Natural Pace**: The audio follows your speed—it never rushes or lags behind.
*   **Smart Focus**: Focuses on interesting landmarks, not just street names.

## 2. Hidden Gems
*   **Local Stories**: Finds the "secret" stories that standard maps miss.
*   **Easy to Find**: Special diamond markers on the map highlight the best local spots.
*   **Deep Dive**: Tap any spot to hear more detail whenever you want.

## 3. Ask Anything (Voice Q&A)
*   **Natural Voice**: Just ask a question like "What is that building?" or "Why is this street famous?"
*   **Smart Answers**: The AI knows your location and answers with local history and facts.
*   **Ready to Help**: Suggests interesting questions if you're not sure what to ask.

## 4. Personalized Stories
*   **Choose Your Style**: Switch between "Story" (immersive), "Facts" (direct), or "Secrets" (hidden gossip).
*   **Your Interests**: Tailor the guide to focus on what you care about most.

## 5. History & Favorites
*   **Walk Summary**: Automatically tracks your distance and the "gems" you've found.
*   **Save for Later**: Keep your favorite stories to listen to again or share.
*   **Easy Restart**: Resume any walk right where you left off.

## 6. Premium Design
*   **Modern Look**: High-quality design inspired by nature and local culture.
*   **Simple to Use**: Clean interface with smooth animations and easy navigation.
