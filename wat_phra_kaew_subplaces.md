# Wat Phra Kaew — Important Sub-places for Demo

These are the primary locations within the Temple of the Emerald Buddha complex to be used for "Sub-Place Selection" and "Gem Discovery" in the demo.

1.  **Phra Ubosot (Chapel of the Emerald Buddha)**
    *   *The heart of the temple.* Houses the Emerald Buddha. High priority for narration.
    *   **Gem Story:** The 1434 lightning strike discovery.

2.  **Phra Sri Rattana Chedi (The Golden Stupa)**
    *   *The towering gold stupa.* Built by King Rama IV.
    *   **Gem Story:** Thousands of golden tiles imported from Italy.

3.  **Phra Mondop (The Library)**
    *   *Green and gold building.* Houses the sacred Tripitaka scriptures.
    *   **Gem Story:** The intricate mother-of-pearl doors.

4.  **Prasat Phra Debidorn (The Royal Pantheon)**
    *   *Cross-shaped building.* Houses life-sized statues of all past kings of the Chakri Dynasty.
    *   **Gem Story:** Only open to the public on specific holidays.

5.  **The Ramakien Gallery (Murals)**
    *   *The surrounding gallery.* Features 178 sections of murals telling the Thai national epic.
    *   **Gem Story:** The delicate restoration process that happens every few years.

6.  **Model of Angkor Wat**
    *   *Historical scale model.* Built during the reign of King Rama IV.
    *   **Gem Story:** Why a Cambodian temple is modeled inside the Thai Grand Palace.

7.  **The Giant Demons (Yaksha Tawiwat)**
    *   *12 towering statues.* Guarding the various gates of the complex.
    *   **Gem Story:** Each demon represents a specific character from the Ramakien.

8.  **Ho Phra Monthien Tham**
    *   *Secondary library.* Used for royal ceremonies and housing ancient Buddhist texts.
